Increase discovery with shoppable photo and videos.

Give your followers an opportunity to buy from every post, story or live video on Facebook and Instagram. Find out how to use tags and stickers to reveal information—like price and a short description—with just a tap.

Make your content shoppable with product tags and stickers.

Tagging your products frequently can help encourage your customers to tap and buy. Followers can click on tags and stickers to view product information and easily make a purchase on your website or directly in-app with checkout. Try it out on different formats such as News Feed on Facebook, and Stories, Reels and IGTV on Instagram.

Build anticipation for a product launch on Instagram.

Businesses that have activated checkout on Instagram1 have access to product launches, which helps them launch new and exclusive products directly on Instagram with features like push notifications and countdown stickers to build anticipation amongst shoppers. A strong product launch strategy encompasses pre-promotion, audience education and creator partnerships to build excitement among followers.

Partner with creators to sell your products on Instagram.

Businesses that have activated checkout on Instagram1 can allow creators and other brands to tag a seller’s products in a post, allowing people to shop inspiring looks from the creators they love without leaving Instagram. Consider giving access to creators you’ve worked with on brand campaigns, PR companies, publications, or friends and family of the brand and employees.

Sell products in real time with Live Shopping.

Live Shopping is a live streamed shopping event that allows you to interact directly with your audience while you showcase and sell your products. Sellers, brands and creators can tag products from their shop or catalog before going live, and swap out tags during the broadcast, so that people can easily tap to learn more and purchase.
